The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a Yurt?
Our luxury yurts are designed to provide a unique, comfortable, and stylish camping experience.
How are the Yurts Finished?
Each yurt is furnished with a queen-sized bed, a kitchenette, and a private deck with a hot tub.
What should I bring?
Pack comfortable clothing, a good book, and a sense of adventure.
